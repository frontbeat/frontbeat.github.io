Frontbeat — The Way to Salvation

Religion cannot save you, for it is written:

"Not by works of righteousness which we have done, but according to his mercy he saved us..." — Titus 3:5 (KJB)

Only faith in Jesus Christ can save, as it says:

"For by grace are ye saved through faith; and that not of yourselves: it is the gift of God:" — Ephesians 2:8 (KJB)
"Jesus saith unto him, I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the Father, but by me." — John 14:6 (KJB)

Trust in Jesus alone for salvation, for He is the only way to eternal life.
